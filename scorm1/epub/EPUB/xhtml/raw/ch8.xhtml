About Our Company

OUR PRIMARY MISSION & VISSION

Our Primary Mission

Our nonprofit organization’s mission statement is broad. We help overcome legal obstacles to share knowledge and creativity around the world. By working closely with major institutions, our vision is an innovative internet that isn’t barred by paywalls. Not only do we thrive to reach the international market, but we also aim to reach every home. Knowledge and creativity are endless. Our ongoing challenges is what makes us powerful.
